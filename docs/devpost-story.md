# Devpost Project Story

## About WindWalker

Building a website is easy. Building an operational business system is hard. Small businesses need functional workflows: booking, checkout, CRM, file intake, customer records, and operator dashboards that can be maintained after launch.

WindWalker is a GPT-powered small-business web app builder that bridges the gap between generated prototypes and working business apps.

## How It Works

**Registry-driven generation.** WindWalker does not start from a blank page. It maps user intent to workflow patterns such as hotel booking, digital checkout, quote pipelines, support tickets, and customer intake.

**Vibe coding with lightweight human support.** Non-technical users can launch with AI. When the app touches payments, bookings, customer data, or operational changes, WindWalker adds a lightweight support loop so the business owner is not left alone.

**Continuous improvement.** Every edit request, deployment result, and support case feeds back into the workflow registry. The system becomes better at turning vague business requests into usable apps.

## Challenges

The main hurdle was not generating screens. The hard part was operational trust. A business owner will not rely on a generated app for payments or bookings unless they know it can be fixed when something goes wrong.

WindWalker addresses this by treating generation as the start of the relationship, not the end. The product combines AI speed with a small-business support model.

## Why OpenAI

GPT can become the interface through which small businesses create and operate software. WindWalker provides the last-mile execution layer: turning GPT-powered conversation into stable, deployed, and supportable business web apps.
